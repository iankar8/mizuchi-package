Frequently Asked Questions

What makes MizuchiAI different from other investment tools?

Unlike traditional stock screeners or research platforms, Mizuchi focuses on organizing your research process and providing AI-powered insights based on your specific context. We don't just provide data—we help you make sense of it in the context of your investment strategy.

How does the AI assistant work?

Mizuchi's AI understands the context of your research and investment goals. You can ask questions in plain English about companies in your watchlist, compare metrics across stocks, or get summaries of recent developments. The more you use Mizuchi, the more it learns about your investment style.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and never store your brokerage credentials. Your research data is encrypted and protected by strict access controls. We're committed to the highest standards of data security and privacy.

Can I collaborate with others while keeping some notes private?

Yes! Mizuchi offers granular sharing controls. You can share entire watchlists or specific stocks while keeping your private notes confidential. This makes it perfect for couples or investment clubs who want to collaborate while maintaining individual perspectives.
